Frequently asked questions

Why does LearningLock ask for Screen Time permission?

LearningLock uses Apple's Screen Time (Family Controls) features to measure time spent in the learning apps a parent approves, and to lock or unlock the reward apps a parent chooses. This permission is required for the app's core feature and is requested only after a parent sets it up.

How do my child's earned minutes work?

A parent sets a multiplier (for example, 1 minute of learning = 10 minutes of reward time). As the child uses an approved learning app, minutes are added to their bank. Those minutes unlock the reward apps until the balance runs out.

Is my child's data private?

Yes. LearningLock stores everything on the device. No usage data, app selections, or personal information is collected, transmitted, or shared. See our Privacy Policy.

Which devices are supported?

LearningLock runs on iPad and iPhone with iOS/iPadOS 16 or later.
